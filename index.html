Project II: Tweet Classifier App

This Twitter Sentiment Classification project aimed to predict an individual’s belief in climate change based on their tweets. This project focused on developing a model that can accurately classify individuals’ climate change beliefs using novel tweet data. Understanding public perception of climate change is essential for companies aiming to reduce environmental impact and carbon footprint as part of their market research. Having completed this classification task effectively, the developed model will enable companies gain valuable consumer sentiment data across various demographics and geographic regions.
